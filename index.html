Project Overview

This demo showcases rapid wireless charging of an RC car using 2D Nano's graphene cement supercapacitors. A Qi transmitter on a spur charges the car when stationary, while a phone Qi pad in the center lets viewers experience universal wireless power.

Live Demo

Watch the RC car complete its lap, then pull off to the charging pad where it instantly tops up its charge. LED indicators reflect capacitor voltage and current in real time.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Graphene Cement Supercaps
  • Capacitance: 10 F
  • Voltage: 5 V
  • 80% Charge: < 10 seconds
  • Qi Frequency: 110–205 kHz
  • LED Feedback: Real-time
